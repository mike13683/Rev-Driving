INTENSIVE COURSE - 5 HOURS

    Suitable for a pupil who just requires a few hours practicing before the test. Ideal if:
  • You failed recently but did well and need a quick re-test.
  • Or have driven illegally and need a brush up on key skills and a quick test.
  • Or have done almost everything as a learner driver but need a quick test.

If you need a theory you would normally do that first and a few days later begin using your 5 hours up & would sit the practical test in the last hour.
